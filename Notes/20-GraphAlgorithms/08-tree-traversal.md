## Notes: Inorder

**Left-Root-Right:** Yields sorted order in binary search trees.

## Notes: Post-Order

**Left-Right-Root:** Used for deleting nodes or evaluating postfix expressions.

## Notes: Pre-Order

**Root-Left-Right:** Used for copying trees or prefix expressions.
